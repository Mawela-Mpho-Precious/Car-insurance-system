Collision Claims

Collision claims cover damage to your vehicle from collisions with another vehicle or object, including single-vehicle accidents like hitting a tree or guardrail.

Comprehensive Claims

Covers damage to your vehicle from non-collision incidents such as theft, vandalism, natural disasters, falling objects, or animal collisions.

Liability Claims

Covers bodily injury or property damage that you cause to another person in an accident.

Personal Injury Protection (PIP) Claims

Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and other related costs for you or your passengers after an accident, regardless of who is at fault.

Glass and Windshield Claims

Repair or replacement of damaged or broken glass, such as windshields, windows, and mirrors.
